Zendo, the inductive logic game

A sequence of playing cards is called a koan. Some koans have the buddha nature, while others do not. The koans appear below. Koans possessing the buddha nature are marked with a check mark, while koans lacking the buddha nature are marked with a cross. Your task is to discern the buddha nature.

You may create your own koans, and ask me to mark them as either having or not having the buddha nature. You may also type the rule which you think is the secret of the buddha nature. If your rule is incorrect, I will aid you by providing a counter-example koan.

You can enter your own koan here, and click "Mark" to learn if it has the buddha nature.

When you think you have comprehended the buddha nature, you may enter your guess here. Begin by typing one of the following words:

a an the one

To be sure that I understand you correctly, are the following paraphrases equivalent to the rule you have in mind? If so, please press ENTER.

  • one of the cards is red
  • at least one of the cards is red
  • one card is a red card
